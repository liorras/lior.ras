This is Lior Rasuli CV-site

Planning and design

UX questions

  1. The target audience of the site:
    Recruiters workers in high-tech companies that specialize in big data analytics.
  2. Main purpose of the site:
    Attract the eye of the user, arouse interest and be neat at first look. third.
  3. Secondary goals:
    - Show all the necessary information about me, with a design focus.
    - Clearly present the ways of communicating with me.
  4. Actions that the user should perform on the site:
    -Click on all the navigation buttons between the different mini-pages that display the various sections in the resume.
    -Use the contact platform by sending me an email.
    -Download my resume file.
  5. Measures for success:
    - Time around of wandering the site, may it won't be short.
    - Receive a personal email from the user to contact for a job.
  6. Content world to which the site refers:
    The site addresses the high-tech world and human resources departments in these companies.

UI questions

  1. Site structure, layout, GRID sketch:
    One page divided in the middle. Top section contains personal image, name, and profession. It has a back cover image.

    The middle and main section of the page contains a structure that varies according to the content into 4 stages:
    -stage 1: WHO AM I - short personal summary.

    -stage 2: Employment Experience (will be presented in the form of a timeline)
    .
    -stage 3: EDUCATION (will be presented in the form of a timeline).

    -stage 4: additional skills - Professional Skillset, Languages. (will be displayed as a percentage)

    -stage 5: Interests, in order to present other aspects of myself that are beyond the field of employment.
    (Will be displayed using circle buttons with a suitable icon. Hovering over the circle will display short text. Clicking on the circle will display a photo or personal video).

    Footer that will display name, email, phone number, link to CONTACT ME page for sending a quick email.
